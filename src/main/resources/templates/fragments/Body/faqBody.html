Frequently Asked Questions

Is CustQr Free to use?

CustQr was is and will always be 100% free to use tool.

Can the Qr's generated from CustQr be used for commercial purpose?

Absolutely. CustQr has zero intention in claiming a copyright towards the generated Qr. Feel free to use it anywhere in your business requirement.

Can I provide non English characters as input while creating Qr code?

Absolutely. CustQr's Qr code creating algorithm is designed to handle all the characters that are UTF-8. Feel free to create your Qr code in your own language for your community.

Can a Qr code be decoded if some part of it was lost?

There is a chance the Qr code might get decoded as the data will be redundant throughout the Qr pattern. However, this depends on how much of the Qr data is lost and the error correction level in which it was generated.

Can Qr codes be customized with any random color?

There isn't any specific color requirement for a Qr code to work. Qr codes can indeed be customized with any random color. However, to maximize the scannability of the Qr code, it is advised to maintain a good amount of contrast with the Qr code's background color and pattern color.

Will the generated Qr code from CustQr get scanned everywhere?

No Qr code generating algorithm can provide 100% guarantee that the resulting Qr will get scanned everywhere, as this depends on the scanning algorithm rather than the generating algorithm.

How efficient is the Qr code from CustQr when it comes to scannability?

I would say, the Qr codes generated from CustQr are 99% scannable in iOS, iPadOS and other apple products Qr code scanning algorithm. In Android smartphones, this would be around 85% as androids struggle with custom shaped Qr code patterns. Other third party scanners might be 80% successful in scanning Qr's from CustQr. Our owndecoding algorithmis also 80% efficient in scanning Qr's from our site. (Our decoding algorithm's are being updated constantly)

I need to generate a Qr code that should have the maximum scannability. What should I do?

In ourQr code creating site, choose the Regular QR option below the Qr Input tab to maximize the scannability factor. Please note that by using this method, no customizations can be done towards the Qr code.

What are the positional markers in the Qr Code?

Positional markers are the boxes that are appear in all corners of the Qr code except bottom-right corner. These act as a guide to the scanning algorithm, facilitating the location of input data in the Qr pattern.
